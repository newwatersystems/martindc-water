Projects

Town of Sterling

This project involves the rehabilitation of an existing valve vault, including the full replacement of all piping and the installation of a new altitude valve. The current piping has deteriorated, and the existing valves are seized, preventing proper operation and maintenance. The new system will ensure reliable water level control, improved functionality, and long-term durability, addressing both operational efficiency and compliance needs.

City of Scobell

This project includes the complete rebuilding of two pump and motors. Additionally, all gaskets and hardware will be replaced to ensure proper sealing and functionality. These upgrades will extend the service life of the pumps and improve the reliability of the water system.

West Test Rural District

This project involves the complete rebuild of a 90 Series Pressure Reducing Valve (PRV) to restore its functionality and extend its service life. By replacing worn components such as diaphragms, seals, and pilot controls, the rebuild eliminates the need for the utility to purchase a costly new valve. This cost-effective solution ensures reliable pressure regulation and reduces long-term system expenses.

Sellers Estates Water System

This project involves servicing a well pump control box to address a blown capacitor and a worn-out contact switch. The repairs include replacing the faulty components to restore proper functionality and ensure reliable pump operation. This maintenance helps prevent further system interruptions and extends the lifespan of the control box.
